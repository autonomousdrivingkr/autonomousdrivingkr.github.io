About me

Hello! I am a Staff Researcher at Samgsung Advanced Institute of Technology (SAIT). Previously, I was a machine learning researcher at Motorola Mobility in Chicago, IL. Before that, I completed my PhD at Purdue University under the supervision of Dr. Karthik Ramani where I studied the interaction between human and machine learning.
My current and past research work broadly involve machine learning, human-computer interaction, visual analytics, and robotics. More recently, I am primarily interested in unsupervised learning, domain generalization, and cross-modal representation learning for computer vision tasks in the field of autonomous driving.
